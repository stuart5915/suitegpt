FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to know how to code?

Nope! That's the whole point. Just describe your app idea in plain english and our AI builds it for you. If you can explain what you want, you can create an app.

How do I get paid?

When users interact with your app, they spend SUITE tokens. You receive a share of those tokens in your wallet. You can withdraw to dollars or crypto anytime.

What kind of apps can I build?

Almost anything! Productivity tools, games, health trackers, social apps, education tools, utilities - if you can describe it, we can build it.

Is there a cost to create?

Creating your first app is free. We only take a small cut (10%) when your app earns money. You keep 90% of everything you make.

How do I update my app?

Just chat with the bot again! Say "update my habit tracker to add dark mode" and we'll push the update. It's that simple.
