About this opportunity

We're looking for motivated individuals to take ownership of apps in the SUITE ecosystem. You run the business and keep 100% of what you earn.

As an App Operator, you'll be responsible for growing your app's user base using our AI-powered marketing tools. We handle all the technical development so you can focus on marketing, community, and growth.

Real ownership, real earnings

You get a business, not a salary. Your earnings scale with your effort. Some apps are already generating revenue while others are waiting for the right person to unlock their potential.

What you'll do

  • Own the growth strategy for your assigned app
  • Create and manage marketing campaigns using Cadence AI
  • Build and engage with your app's user community
  • Submit feature requests and improvements through Factory
  • Track performance and optimize for growth

What you get

  • 100% revenue share - You keep everything your app earns
  • 10,000 SUITE credits/month - Marketing budget for ads and promotion
  • AI marketing tools - Cadence AI for content creation and scheduling
  • Development support - Our team builds features you request
  • Full analytics - Dashboard to track users, revenue, and growth
  • Community - Connect with other operators and the dev team

What we're looking for

  • Self-motivated and comfortable working independently
  • Interest in marketing, growth, or community building
  • Willingness to commit 10+ hours per week
  • No coding skills required - we handle all technical work
  • Entrepreneurial mindset - you want to build something

How it works

  • Apply - Tell us which app interests you and why
  • Review - We review applications and reach out via email
  • Onboard - Get access to your app, dashboard, and marketing tools
  • Grow - Start building your app's user base and earning revenue
