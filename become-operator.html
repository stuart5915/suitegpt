About this opportunity

We're looking for people to supervise AI bots that run apps in the SUITE ecosystem. You're not doing the work - you're managing the AI that does. Think of it as being on-call to approve what the bot wants to do.

Each app has an AI bot assigned to it that handles marketing, content creation, and engagement. Your job is to review the bot's proposed actions in the Governance Dashboard and say yes or no. The AI does the heavy lifting - you provide human oversight.

Supervise an AI, earn 100% revenue

This isn't a traditional job with hours and tasks. You're on-call to respond to your AI bot's prompts - approve posts, review campaigns, make quick decisions. The more you let the AI work, the more your app grows, the more you earn.

What you'll do

  • Approve AI prompts - Your bot proposes posts, campaigns, and actions. You say yes or no.
  • Monitor the Governance Dashboard - Check in when the bot needs your input
  • Set high-level direction - Tell the AI what tone, audience, or goals to focus on
  • Review performance - See what's working and adjust the AI's parameters
  • Be on-call - Respond to bot prompts within a reasonable timeframe

What you get

  • 100% revenue share - You keep everything your app earns
  • Your own AI bot - A dedicated AI agent that runs your app's marketing
  • Governance Dashboard - Clean interface to review and approve bot actions
  • Minimal time commitment - Just respond to prompts when the bot needs input
  • Full analytics - See what your bot is doing and how the app is performing
  • Development support - Our team builds features you request through Factory

What we're looking for

  • Responsive - Can check in and approve prompts regularly
  • Good judgment - Know when to say yes or no to AI suggestions
  • Comfortable with AI - Understand that you're supervising, not doing
  • No coding required - The bot and our team handle all technical work
  • Ownership mindset - Treat the app like it's yours (because it is)

How it works

  • Apply - Tell us which app interests you and why
  • Review - We review applications and reach out via email
  • Onboard - Get access to your app's AI bot and Governance Dashboard
  • Supervise - Approve AI actions, watch your app grow, earn revenue
