Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need coding skills?
No! App Operators focus on marketing, community building, and growth. Our dev team handles all technical work. You submit feature requests through Factory, and we build them.
How does the revenue share work?
You keep 90% of all revenue your app generates (subscriptions, in-app purchases, ad revenue). SUITE takes 10% to cover infrastructure, development, and support costs.
What's the marketing budget for?
You receive 10,000 SUITE credits monthly to run ads through Cadence AI. This covers social media ads, influencer outreach, and content promotion. Unused credits roll over.
Can I operate multiple apps?
Possibly! If you're doing great with one app and we have apps that need operators, we can discuss expanding. Quality over quantity though.
What if the app isn't making money yet?
That's normal for new apps. Your job is to grow the user base and find product-market fit. The marketing budget helps you experiment. Many apps take time to monetize.
