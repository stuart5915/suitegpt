Frequently Asked Questions

Is my resume data secure?
Your resume PDF is processed entirely in your browser using pdf.js. The raw file never leaves your device. Only the extracted text is sent for AI analysis over an encrypted connection. We don't store your resume.
How is the match score calculated?
AI compares your skills, experience, education, and keywords against the job requirements, responsibilities, and qualifications. The score reflects how well your background aligns with what the employer is looking for.
Can I use the rewritten bullets directly?
Yes! The rewritten bullets and tailored summary are designed to be copied directly into your resume. The cover letter is a complete draft ready to customize with personal touches.
Does it work for any industry?
Yes. ResumeForge works with any job posting and resume — tech, finance, healthcare, education, marketing, engineering, and more. It adapts to the specific terminology and requirements of each role.
