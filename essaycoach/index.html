Frequently Asked Questions

Is my essay stored?
No. Your essay is processed in real-time for feedback and is not stored on our servers. Each session starts fresh.
Will this write my essay for me?
No. EssayCoach reviews and improves what you've already written. It gives feedback, fixes, and suggestions — but the writing is yours.
How is the score calculated?
AI evaluates five dimensions: clarity, structure, grammar, argument strength, and style. The overall score is a weighted average, with contextual adjustments based on essay type.
Does it work for non-English essays?
It works best with English essays. For other languages, basic grammar and structure feedback is available but may be less accurate.
How much does it cost?
