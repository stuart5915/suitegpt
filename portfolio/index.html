About

Builder First, Strategist Always

I build AI products and ship them. My flagship project, SuiteGPT, is a full AI app platform with 20+ live tools — built from scratch with Claude, Gemini, and Groq APIs, a Supabase backend, Vercel deployment, and a governance system. I designed the product architecture, credit system, cross-domain auth, iframe sandboxing, and builder economics.

Before AI, I spent 7+ years in DeFi and blockchain — writing foundational whitepapers (MELD on Cardano), managing marketing for multiple protocols, directing operations for RWA tokenization, and building DeFi education programs. I know how to take complex technical systems and make them accessible.

My career started in mechatronics engineering — programming CNC machinery in Java, Python, and C++. That systems-thinking foundation carries through everything I build. I’m looking for a role at an AI company where I can combine product instinct, technical depth, and communication skills to help ship great products.
