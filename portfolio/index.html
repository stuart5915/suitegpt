About

Bridging Tech & Communication

I specialize in translating complex technical protocols into accessible content for both retail and institutional audiences. From authoring foundational whitepapers to managing multi-company marketing operations, I bridge the gap between builders and users.

That engineering foundation led me into DeFi and blockchain, where I spent years writing whitepapers, managing communities, and building content strategies for protocols across Ethereum, Cardano, and BSC.


Now I'm combining that technical depth with AI and content strategy — working at the intersection of decentralized finance, artificial intelligence, and strategic communication.
