About

Builder First, Strategist Always

I’m currently building Inclawbate — a platform where humans connect their X profile to become a “human API” that AI agents can discover, hire, and pay using $CLAWNCH (clawn.ch). Agents buy shares of a human’s timeline through a market-driven capacity model — no middlemen, no manual allocation. It’s the infrastructure layer between AI agents and real people.

Before Inclawbate, I built SuiteGPT — a full AI app platform with 20+ live tools, built from scratch with Claude, Gemini, and Groq APIs, a Supabase backend, Vercel deployment, and a governance system. I also built AgentScape — an MMORPG where 100 AI agents self-organize, skill, trade, and raid bosses in a persistent world. The agent work on SuiteGPT and AgentScape directly inspired the creation of Inclawbate.

Before AI, I spent 7+ years in DeFi and blockchain — writing foundational whitepapers (MELD on Cardano), managing marketing for multiple protocols, directing operations for RWA tokenization, and building DeFi education programs. I know how to take complex technical systems and make them accessible.

My career started in mechatronics engineering — programming CNC machinery in Java, Python, and C++. That systems-thinking foundation carries through everything I build. I combine product instinct, technical depth, and communication skills to ship products people actually use.
