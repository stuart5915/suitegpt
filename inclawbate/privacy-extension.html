What the extension does

Inclawbator is a Chrome extension that generates AI-powered reply suggestions on X (Twitter). It reads the text of tweets you interact with and sends that text to our server to generate a reply.

Data we collect

  • Tweet text: The text of the tweet you're replying to is sent to our API to generate a reply. It is not stored after the response is generated.
  • API key: Your API key is stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage.sync) to authenticate requests. It is never shared with third parties.
  • Extension settings: Your tone, persona, and other preferences are stored locally in your browser and sent with each request to personalize replies.

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect browsing history
  • We do not collect personal information beyond your X handle (used for authentication)
  • We do not track your activity on X
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels

Permissions explained

  • storage: To save your API key and settings locally
  • activeTab: To read the tweet text on the current page when you click the generate button
  • host_permissions (x.com, twitter.com): To inject the reply generation button on tweets
  • host_permissions (inclawbate.com): To auto-connect your account after X OAuth

Third-party services

Generated replies are powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Tweet text sent for reply generation is subject to Anthropic's privacy policy.
