How It Works

Four Phases. Zero Dead Time.

Inclawbate guides every token through a structured incubation process. Nothing launches naked into the void.

1

Concept

Describe your token idea. An agent explores the concept, generates brand options — name, ticker, colors, tagline, narrative. You pick the winner.

2

Build

The agent prepares every launch asset: landing page, social profiles, content calendar, DexScreener profile, and the formatted launch post. All staged for review.

3

Launch

One button. Token deploys on Base via Uniswap V4, landing page goes live, socials fire — everything simultaneously. Tradeable in 60 seconds.

4

Grow

Post-launch, your agent handles ongoing marketing, analytics monitoring, fee claiming (80% of swap fees in WETH), and community building.
