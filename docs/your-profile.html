Your Profile

Connect your wallet and Telegram to access all SUITE features.

Ways to Connect

SUITE supports two connection methods. You can use one or both:

🔗
Wallet
Connect any Ethereum-compatible wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, etc.) on the Base network. Required for buying credits and withdrawing USDC.
✉
Telegram
Link your Telegram account to vote on polls, submit app ideas, and receive notifications about your credits and requests.

Connecting Your Wallet

1
Go to Profile Click "Profile" in the navigation bar
2
Click Connect Wallet Choose your wallet provider (MetaMask, Coinbase, etc.)
3
Approve Connection Confirm the connection in your wallet app
4
Switch to Base If prompted, switch to the Base network

Need Base Network?

SUITE runs on Base (Coinbase's L2). If you don't have Base configured, your wallet will prompt you to add it. Base has very low fees - typically less than $0.01 per transaction.

Linking Telegram

1
Click Link Telegram From your profile page, click the "Link" button next to Telegram
2
Open Telegram A Telegram login widget will appear - click to open Telegram
3
Authorize SUITE Confirm that SUITE can see your Telegram username

Managing Connections

You can disconnect either connection at any time from your profile:

  • Disconnect Wallet - Click "Disconnect" next to your wallet address. Your credits remain on-chain and will reappear when you reconnect.
  • Unlink Telegram - Click "Unlink" next to your Telegram handle. You can re-link anytime.
Your data is safe: Disconnecting doesn't delete your credits or account. Your on-chain balance persists, and you can reconnect anytime to access it.
