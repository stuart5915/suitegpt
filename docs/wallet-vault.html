How to Deposit

1

Connect Wallet

Click "Connect Wallet" and select your wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase, etc.). Make sure you're on Base network.

2

Click Deposit

Click the "Deposit" button to open the deposit modal.

3

Choose Asset & Amount

Select ETH or USDC and enter the amount. Use the +/- buttons for quick adjustments.

4

Confirm Transaction

Confirm the transaction in your wallet. After confirmation, SUITE tokens will appear in your balance.

üõ°Ô∏è Floor Price Guarantee

Every SUITE token is backed by real ETH in the Treasury. You can always redeem at the floor price, no matter what happens on DEX markets. The price can go up, but never below floor.

How to Withdraw

1

Click Withdraw

Click the "Withdraw" or "Cash Out" button to open the withdrawal modal.

2

Enter Amount

Enter the amount of SUITE to redeem. You'll see the ETH value you'll receive (minus 0.5% fee).

3

Confirm Transaction

Confirm the burn transaction. SUITE is burned and ETH is sent to your wallet.

üìä Your Yield Destination
