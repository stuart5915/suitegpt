Bonus Credits & Rewards

When you buy credits, you also receive bonus credits as a thank-you. These bonus credits can be withdrawn back to USDC if you decide you don't need them.

The Redemption Rate

Your bonus credits have a redemption rate - the percentage of face value you can withdraw. This rate changes based on how the SUITE reward pool performs. It might be 80%, 100%, or even higher.

No Subscriptions

SUITE doesn't do monthly subscriptions. Here's why:

  • Pay for what you use - Credits only spent when you actually use features
  • No pressure - No "use it or lose it" monthly quota
  • Credits don't expire - Buy once, use whenever
  • Works across all apps - One balance, everywhere

Free Tier

Every app has free features. Basic tracking, logging, and viewing is always free. AI-powered features use credits.

Try Before You Buy

New users get free credits to try premium features. No credit card required to start using SUITE apps.
