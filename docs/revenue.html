💰 Revenue & Fees

Complete breakdown of how SUITE generates revenue and where fees are collected throughout the ecosystem.

🎯 Core Principle: Transparent & Sustainable

All fees go toward sustaining the ecosystem, rewarding creators, and charitable giving. 10% of all treasury yield goes to community-voted charities.

📋 Fee Structure Overview

Action Fee Where It Goes
Deposit ETH → Mint SUITE 0.5% Treasury (backs all SUITE)
Redeem SUITE → ETH 0.5% Treasury
AI Action (per use) 1 SUITE 70% Creator, 30% Treasury
Store Purchase 0% 100% to product pool
Ad Watch Reward +2 SUITE User earns from ad revenue

🔄 Revenue Streams

SUITE generates revenue from multiple sources that flow into the treasury:

📺 Ad Revenue

When users watch ads in SUITE apps, advertisers pay us. This revenue is deposited into the treasury and users receive SUITE credits.

Rate: ~$0.002 per ad view → 2 SUITE to user

💳 Store Purchases

Users buy boosts, priority queue slots, and premium features using SUITE. This SUITE gets burned or recycled.

Rate: Products priced in SUITE, recycled to ecosystem

🎮 In-App Usage

Every AI action (meal scan, workout generation, etc.) costs 1 SUITE. Creators earn 70%, treasury gets 30%.

Rate: 1 SUITE per AI action

📈 Treasury Yield

Treasury ETH is deployed to DeFi strategies (staking, lending) generating passive yield that increases the floor price.

Rate: Variable (3-8% APY typical)

💸 Where Money Flows

USER ACTION → Fee → Destination ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────── Deposit 1 ETH to Treasury → 0.5% → Treasury (0.995 ETH) → → User gets SUITE at floor price Use AI Feature (1 SUITE) → 100% → 70% App Creator → → 30% Treasury Watch Ad → $0 → User earns 2 SUITE → Ad $ → Treasury (backs new SUITE) Cash Out SUITE → ETH → 0.5% → Treasury (kept as fee) → → User gets proportional ETH Treasury Yield (Monthly) → 90% → Stays in treasury (floor↑) → 10% → Charity giving pool

👨‍💻 Creator Revenue Split

When users pay SUITE to use your app's AI features:

Recipient Share Notes
App Creator 70% Paid directly to your Discord/wallet
Platform (Treasury) 30% Funds infrastructure, AI costs, development

Example: Your App Gets 1,000 Uses

1,000 uses × 1 SUITE = 1,000 SUITE spent

  • You earn: 700 SUITE (70%)
  • Platform keeps: 300 SUITE (30%)

At floor price of $0.001/SUITE = $0.70 to you

At premium DEX price of $0.01/SUITE = $7.00 to you

🎁 Charity Giving (10%)

10% of all treasury yield is allocated to the Giving Pool. SUITE holders vote monthly on which charities receive funds.

  • Voting Power: Based on SUITE held/staked
  • Distribution: Monthly, on-chain
  • Transparency: All donations publicly verifiable

🔥 Fee-Free Actions

Not everything costs money! These actions are completely free:

  • ✅ Creating an account
  • ✅ First 20 AI actions (free tier)
  • ✅ Watching ads to earn SUITE
  • ✅ Voting on charities
  • ✅ Transferring credits between Discord accounts
  • ✅ Browsing and downloading SUITE apps
  • ✅ Submitting app ideas

⛽ Gas Costs (Blockchain)

For on-chain actions on Base network:

Action Typical Gas Cost
Deposit ETH to Treasury ~$0.01 - $0.05
Withdraw from Treasury ~$0.01 - $0.05
Transfer SUITE (ERC-20) ~$0.005 - $0.02
Claim NFT Boost ~$0.02 - $0.10

Gas costs vary with network congestion. Base is typically very cheap!

💡 Summary

For Users: Watch ads = free SUITE. Small fees only when converting to/from ETH.
