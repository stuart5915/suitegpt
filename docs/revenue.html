Yield Distribution

When you deposit to the vault, you choose how to allocate your yield:

  • Minimum 10% to AI Fleet — Required contribution to fund autonomous app development
  • Up to 90% to yourself — Keep the majority of your yield

Example: $10,000 Deposit at 10% APY

Your deposit: $10,000 USDC Vault APY: 10% Annual yield: $1,000 Your allocation: 80% to self, 20% to AI Fleet Your earnings: $800/year AI Fleet receives: $200/year Principal always safe: $10,000 (withdraw anytime)

Revenue Sources

The AI Fleet generates revenue through multiple channels:

App Subscriptions
Users subscribe to premium features in AI Fleet apps. Revenue flows back to the vault.
Credit Purchases
Direct credit pack purchases fund ongoing operations and AI infrastructure costs.
Ad Revenue
Users can watch ads to earn free credits. Advertisers pay, users and platform both benefit.
Enterprise Licensing
B2B licensing of AI Fleet apps to businesses generates larger contract revenue.

The Flywheel Effect

How It Compounds

More deposits → More yield → More AI Fleet funding → More apps built → More revenue → Higher returns → More deposits

The ecosystem becomes self-sustaining as app revenue supplements and eventually exceeds depositor yield contributions.

Free Actions

Not everything costs money. These are completely free:

  • Creating an account
  • First 20 app uses (free tier)
  • Watching ads to earn credits
  • Browsing and downloading apps
  • Participating in governance
  • Daily check-in rewards
  • Referring friends
