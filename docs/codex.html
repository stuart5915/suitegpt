The Method: Ontological Design

How does one generate new incentive systems, new connections, new worlds ‚Äî all founded in coherent meaning? Here is the process:

Externalize the Implicit

Take what exists only in your mind and make it visible. Write down every entity, every concept, every named thing. Put it on paper, on screen, on a wall. The act of externalization is itself a creative act.

Contemplate with Stillness

Sit with your externalized map. Don't force connections. Adopt a posture of openness and let patterns emerge. The stillness is not passive ‚Äî it's an atmosphere of intense potential where intuitions arise.

Draw New Connections

As you see the whole, new relationships reveal themselves. "What if this connected to that?" "What if there was a word for this gap?" These intuitions are the generative engine of the system.

Name the New Things

When you see a pattern that needs a name, name it. Choose words that carry weight, that evoke feeling, that will stick in memory. TELOS. FORGE. Cadence. Treasury. These aren't just labels ‚Äî they're invocations.

Recurse

The new names create new space for more naming. The map grows. The world deepens. Return to step one with your expanded ontology. Each cycle adds coherence and meaning.
