FAQ

How much time does this take?

It's on-call, not hours-based. Your AI bot does the work. You just need to check in periodically to approve prompts - could be a few minutes a day or more depending on how active your bot is.

What does the AI bot actually do?

The bot handles marketing: writing posts, scheduling content, engaging with users, running campaigns. It proposes actions and you approve them in the Governance Dashboard.

Why does SUITE give 100% revenue?

Our business model is based on credits, not app revenue. When you grow your app, more users join the ecosystem and buy credits. Everyone wins.

Can I supervise multiple bots?

Yes, depending on availability. If you prove yourself with one app, you can take on more bots to supervise.
