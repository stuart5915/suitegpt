FAQ

How much can I earn?

It depends on the app and your effort. Some apps already have users generating revenue. Others are waiting for the right operator to unlock their potential. You keep 100% of whatever the app earns.

Why does SUITE give 100% revenue?

Our business model is based on credits, not app revenue. When you grow your app, more users join the ecosystem and buy credits. Everyone wins.

Can I operate multiple apps?

Yes, depending on availability. If you prove yourself with one app, you can take on more.

What if I need a feature built?

Submit requests through Factory. Our dev team prioritizes operator requests and builds what you need.
