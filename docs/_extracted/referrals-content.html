FAQ

When do I get credited?

Credits are awarded the moment your friend's deposit transaction confirms on-chain. This typically takes 5-15 seconds on Base.

What's the minimum deposit?

Your friend must deposit at least $5 worth of ETH or USDC for you to earn credits. This prevents gaming with dust deposits.

Can I refer myself?

No. Self-referrals are automatically blocked. The system checks if the referrer and referee are the same Discord ID or wallet address.

Is there a limit?

There's no hard limit on referrals. Refer as many friends as you want!

What if my friend deposits multiple times?

You only earn once per friend. The reward is based on their first qualifying deposit that crosses the $5 threshold.
