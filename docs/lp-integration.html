How Price Works

SUITE has a floor price guaranteed by the Treasury (Treasury Value √∑ Total Supply). The DEX price floats based on supply and demand:

  • üìà DEX > Floor - Buy from Treasury, sell on DEX = Profit (arbitrage)
  • üìä DEX = Floor - Markets are in equilibrium
  • üìâ DEX < Floor - Can't happen! Arbitrageurs would buy DEX, redeem at Treasury

‚ö° Community Arbitrage

When DEX price is above floor, anyone can execute arbitrage through the Vault. Buy from Treasury at floor, sell on DEX at market price. You keep 50% of the spread, 50% goes to Treasury!

Providing Liquidity

1

Go to Aerodrome

Visit aerodrome.finance and connect your wallet on Base.

2

Find SUITE/ETH Pool

Navigate to Pools and search for SUITE/ETH. If not listed, add SUITE by contract address.

3

Add Liquidity

Deposit equal value of SUITE and ETH. You'll receive LP tokens representing your share.

4

Earn Fees

As people trade, you earn 0.3% of every swap proportional to your LP share.
