🗺️ SUITE Roadmap

Where we're headed. Built in public, evolved by the community.

🌟 The Vision

SUITE is building a perpetual, community-owned ecosystem where apps generate value, treasury grows through yield, and governance evolves from solo founder to full DAO.

📍 Phase 1: Foundation (Now)

Current
Launch & Liquidity
  • ✅ Treasury deployed on Base mainnet (TreasuryV4)
  • ✅ SUITE token with proportional backing
  • ✅ Wallet page with ETH/USDC deposits
  • 🔄 LP pool on Aerodrome (ETH/SUITE)
  • 🔄 8 apps finalized to production
  • 🔄 Discord commands enabled

🏦 Phase 2: Treasury Evolution

Q1 2026
Yield & Transparency
  • 🔐 Gnosis Safe multi-sig (solo admin → trusted signers)
  • 📊 Treasury dashboard with live allocations
  • 💰 Yield strategies (Aave, Lido, lending pools)
  • 🎯 Signer skin-in-game: Multi-sig signers must lock SUITE/ETH
  • 📢 Weekly treasury updates in Discord
  • 💱 Dynamic pricing: USD-denominated fees paid in SUITE (oracle-based)

🗳️ Phase 3: Community Governance

Q2 2026
DAO & Voting
  • 🗳️ Snapshot voting for treasury decisions
  • 💡 Community proposals for yield allocation
  • 🤝 Multi-sig expansion (3-of-5, 5-of-7)
  • ❤️ Charity/giving allocation votes
  • 🏛️ Finance section in Discord for treasury enthusiasts

🚀 Phase 4: Expansion

2026+
Scale & Reach
  • 📱 Native mobile apps (TestFlight, Play Store)
  • 🤖 Agentic AI app development
  • 🪙 Token listings (CEX/DEX expansion)
  • ⛓️ Multi-chain deployment (Ethereum, Arbitrum, Optimism)
  • 🌍 Global community & localization

🔮 Long-term Vision

SUITE becomes a perpetual, self-sustaining ecosystem:

  • Treasury generates yield that raises the floor forever
  • Apps generate revenue that flows to treasury
  • Community governs allocation of yield to building, giving, growing
  • A vibe coding team ready to build whatever comes next
