Every SUITE token is backed by real ETH in the treasury. This creates a hard price floor that makes it structurally impossible for SUITE to trade below its backing value.

üîë Key Insight: It's Structurally Impossible

Every SUITE token in existence was either:

  • Minted by depositing ETH to the treasury (at floor price)

No one would ever sell below floor because they can always redeem at the treasury for floor price!

üìà Why Price Can Go UP

While the floor provides downside protection, SUITE can trade above the floor on DEX markets due to:

  • Demand: More people wanting to use SUITE apps
  • Scarcity: Limited supply on DEX
  • Speculation: Traders expecting future growth
  • Buybacks: Treasury using yield to buy SUITE

üè¶ Treasury Growth

The treasury grows through:

  • Deposits: Users depositing ETH to mint SUITE
  • Fees: Small fees on deposits/withdrawals
  • Yield: Treasury ETH earning staking rewards

As treasury grows faster than supply (thanks to fees), the floor price increases over time!
