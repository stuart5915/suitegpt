📊 SUITE Tokenomics

How SUITE maintains its value and why the price can never fall below the floor.

🛡️ The Floor Price Guarantee

Every SUITE token is backed by real ETH in the treasury. This creates a hard price floor that makes it structurally impossible for SUITE to trade below its backing value.

🔑 Key Insight: It's Structurally Impossible

Every SUITE token in existence was either:

  • Minted by depositing ETH to the treasury (at floor price)
  • Bought from DEX at market price (at or above floor)

No one would ever sell below floor because they can always redeem at the treasury for floor price!

💰 How Treasury Backing Works

User deposits 1 ETH to Treasury ↓ Treasury mints SUITE at floor price ↓ User receives SUITE tokens ↓ Floor Price = Treasury ETH / Total SUITE Supply ↓ User can ALWAYS redeem SUITE for proportional ETH

📈 Why Price Can Go UP

While the floor provides downside protection, SUITE can trade above the floor on DEX markets due to:

  • Demand: More people wanting to use SUITE apps
  • Scarcity: Limited supply on DEX
  • Speculation: Traders expecting future growth
  • Buybacks: Treasury using yield to buy SUITE

🏦 Treasury Growth

The treasury grows through:

  • Deposits: Users depositing ETH to mint SUITE
  • Fees: Small fees on deposits/withdrawals
  • Yield: Treasury ETH earning staking rewards

As treasury grows faster than supply (thanks to fees), the floor price increases over time!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use SUITE as a hedge against ETH price drops?

Not exactly. SUITE is backed by ETH in the treasury, so if ETH drops 50%, your SUITE redemption value drops proportionally. However, SUITE can trade above the treasury floor on external DEXs if there's demand for the utility. The treasury creates a guaranteed floor price - SUITE can never go to zero because there's always backing.

How does the treasury floor price work?

Floor Price = Treasury Value ÷ Total SUITE Supply
If the treasury has $100k and 100M SUITE exists, floor = $0.001 per SUITE. When you redeem, you always get your proportional share of the treasury. On DEXs, the price can be higher (demand for utility) but not lower (arbitrage would correct it).

How much SUITE do I earn from watching ads?

You earn 2 SUITE per ad view. This is calculated to match the ~$0.002 ad revenue deposited into the treasury, ensuring the system stays solvent. Watch more ads = earn more SUITE = more AI actions in apps!

What's the difference between credits and on-chain SUITE?

Credits = Off-chain, instant, stored in our database. Great for quick actions.
On-chain SUITE = Real ERC-20 tokens on Base. Tradeable, verifiable, truly yours.
You can convert credits to on-chain SUITE anytime using the /onchain command in Discord.

What happens if everyone redeems at once?

The treasury is designed for proportional redemption. If 10% of SUITE holders redeem, they get 10% of the treasury. The remaining holders still have 100% of their proportional share. The system stays solvent because you can only withdraw what you're entitled to - never more.

How does SUITE generate yield?

The treasury ETH is deployed to DeFi yield strategies (staking, lending, LP positions). Yield earned increases the total treasury value, which increases the floor price of all SUITE tokens. You benefit just by holding!

Is there a fee for minting or redeeming?

Minting: 0.5% fee goes to community treasury for development.
Redeeming: 0.5% fee + gas costs on Base (~$0.01).
These small fees help sustain the ecosystem while keeping transactions affordable.
