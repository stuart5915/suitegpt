How It Works

The SUITE Vault is a non-custodial DeFi vault that connects directly to Yearn Finance. Your funds are never held by SUITE ‚Äî they're always in the Yearn vault earning yield.

1
Deposit USDC
Connect your wallet and deposit USDC. The vault immediately deposits your funds into Yearn's USDC vault.
2
Receive SUITE
You receive SUITE tokens representing your share of the vault. As Yearn earns yield, your SUITE becomes worth more USDC.
3
Earn Yield
Yearn's strategies automatically generate yield. No action needed ‚Äî your SUITE appreciates in value over time.
4
Withdraw Anytime
Burn your SUITE to withdraw proportional USDC at any time. No lockups, no waiting periods.
