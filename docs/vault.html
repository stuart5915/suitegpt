Reward Pool

How bonus credits work and what backs their value.

The Simple Explanation

When you buy credits, you get bonus credits on top. These bonus credits can be withdrawn for USDC. How much you get back depends on how the reward pool is doing.

How It Works

1
Buy Credits
Pay $1 in USDC and receive 1,000 credits to use in apps. You also get bonus credits as a reward.
2
Funds Go to Work
Your USDC goes into strategies that generate returns - yield farming, trading, and other opportunities.
3
Pool Grows (or Shrinks)
The reward pool value changes based on how these strategies perform. It could grow significantly or decrease.
4
Redemption Rate Updates
The rate shows what percentage of face value you can withdraw. 100% = break even, higher = profit, lower = loss.

Understanding the Rate

What the Redemption Rate Means

Rate = 120% → Your bonus credits are worth 20% MORE than face value

Rate = 100% → Your bonus credits are worth exactly face value

Rate = 80% → Your bonus credits are worth 20% LESS than face value

What Affects the Rate

Rate Goes Up When...
  • Yield strategies perform well
  • Trading profits are realized
  • App revenue flows in
Rate Goes Down When...
  • Market conditions hurt returns
  • Strategies underperform
  • More bonus credits are issued

Important Notes

This Is Not Guaranteed

The reward pool value can go up or down. There's no guaranteed return. Only withdraw if the current rate works for you, or wait for it to improve.

What You Can Withdraw

Only bonus credits can be withdrawn. Credits you purchase directly are for using apps - they're spent when you use AI features and can't be withdrawn.

Withdrawal Process

1
Request Submit a withdrawal request for the amount you want
2
Queue Your request enters the queue while funds are prepared
3
Claim When ready, claim your USDC to your wallet
