Quadratic Voting

SUITE uses quadratic voting to keep things fair. The more votes you cast on a single proposal, the more each additional vote costs:

Vote Cost Calculator

1st vote = FREE 2nd vote = 1 credit (1 total) 3rd vote = 2 credits (3 total) 4th vote = 3 credits (6 total) 5th vote = 4 credits (10 total) Formula: cost(n) = n √ó (n-1) / 2

Proposal Lifecycle

1. Submitted

Anyone with a connected account can submit a proposal. Describe what you want built or changed.

2. Open Voting

When the first vote is cast, a 7-day voting period begins. Community members spend credits to vote for or against. Everyone gets 1 free vote per proposal.

3. Resolution

After voting ends, proposals are reviewed. Passed proposals enter the AI Fleet's build queue. Authors of passed proposals earn bonus credits.

Why This System?

Skin in the Game
Voting costs real credits, so people only vote on things they actually care about. No spam, no noise.
Benefits Everyone
Credits spent on voting boost the redemption rate for all credit holders. Voting is a contribution.

Current Phase: Founder Stewardship

Building the Foundation

SUITE is currently in the Founder Stewardship phase. This means:

  • The founder maintains oversight of final decisions
  • Community input shapes priorities through voting
  • Governance rules are being refined based on real usage
  • All changes are documented publicly

Voting Rules

  • First vote is free ‚Äî Everyone gets 1 free vote per proposal
  • One direction per proposal ‚Äî Once you vote FOR or AGAINST, you can't switch
  • Add more votes anytime ‚Äî You can increase your vote power (costs more credits)
  • All votes are public ‚Äî Governance data is fully transparent
