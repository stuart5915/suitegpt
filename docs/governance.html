Governance

How depositors shape the future of SUITE.

Simple Rule

Your deposit amount = your voting power. The more you deposit to the USDC vault, the more influence you have over the AI Fleet's direction.

Who Can Vote?

Governance is open to all USDC vault depositors. Your voting power is proportional to your deposit:

Voting Power Example

Total vault deposits: $100,000 USDC Your deposit: $5,000 USDC Your voting power: 5% Another depositor: $20,000 USDC Their voting power: 20%

How Voting Works

1. Proposal Created

Anyone can submit a proposal. For app concepts, the AI Fleet automatically generates proposals when it has new ideas ready for review.

2. Voting Period

Proposals are open for voting for 7 days. Connect your wallet and cast your vote (YES or NO). Voting is gasless ‚Äî just sign a message to prove ownership.

3. Execution

If a proposal passes (majority YES weighted by deposit amount), it moves to implementation. App concepts go into the AI Fleet build queue.

Proposal Categories

Category Threshold Duration
App Concepts Simple majority (>50%) 7 days
Feature Requests Simple majority (>50%) 7 days
Yield Strategy Supermajority (>66%) 14 days
Major Changes Supermajority (>66%) 14 days

Feature Requests

Depositors can submit feature requests for existing apps or new capabilities:

  • Describe the feature you want
  • Tag the relevant app (FoodVitals, OpticRep, etc.) or mark as "Platform"
  • Community votes on priority
  • Top-voted features go into the AI Fleet's queue

Your Voice Matters

Unlike traditional app companies where decisions happen behind closed doors, SUITE depositors directly shape what gets built. Your yield allocation funds development, and your votes decide direction.

Security

  • Wallet Verification: All votes require wallet signature (gasless)
  • Snapshot Voting: Your voting power is based on deposit at proposal creation
  • No Double Voting: One vote per wallet per proposal
  • Transparent Results: All votes are publicly viewable

Get Started

  1. Deposit USDC to the vault
  2. Your voting power activates immediately
  3. Browse active proposals and cast your votes
  4. Submit your own proposals or feature requests
