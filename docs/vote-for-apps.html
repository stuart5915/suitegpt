What Makes a Good App Idea?

✓
Solves a Real Problem
The best app ideas address something people actually struggle with. "I wish there was an app that..." is a great starting point.
✓
Simple & Focused
Apps that do one thing well win over complex multi-feature apps. Think "track my water intake" not "manage my entire life."
✓
AI Can Help
SUITE apps leverage AI. Ideas where AI adds value (analysis, recommendations, automation) are prioritized.
✓
Broad Appeal
Apps that help many people tend to win polls. Niche ideas are great too, but may take longer to get built.
