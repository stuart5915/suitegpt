What is SUITE?

SUITE is an autonomous AI-powered ecosystem that builds, deploys, and operates micro-apps 24/7. Users can earn yield by depositing into the USDC vault, which funds the AI Fleet's operations.

Key Concepts

AI Fleet - Autonomous AI agents that build apps around the clock. The factory never sleeps.

USDC Vault - Deposit USDC and earn yield. A portion of your yield funds the AI Fleet.

SUITE Credits - In-app currency used across all SUITE apps. Earned through various activities.

Apps - AI-built micro-apps solving real problems. FoodVitals, OpticRep, TrueForm, and more.

How It Works

The Vision: SUITE's AI Fleet autonomously researches market gaps, designs apps, builds them through iterative refinement, and deploys them ‚Äî all without human intervention. Depositors fund this operation and share in the upside.

The USDC Vault

When you deposit USDC into the vault:

  1. Deposit - Your USDC goes into a yield-generating vault
  2. Yield Allocation - Choose how much yield goes to AI Fleet vs. yourself (min 10% to fleet)
  3. Earn - Your principal stays safe, yield accumulates based on your allocation
  4. Withdraw - Get back your principal + your share of yield anytime
