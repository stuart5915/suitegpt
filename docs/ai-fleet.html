How It Works

1. Market Research

The AI continuously analyzes existing app stores, user reviews, and market trends to identify gaps and opportunities. It looks for:

  • Underserved niches with demand
  • Apps with poor reviews that could be improved
  • Emerging trends without adequate tools
  • Cross-category opportunities

2. Concept Generation

Once a gap is identified, the AI generates an app concept including:

  • Name and branding
  • Core features and user flows
  • Target audience
  • Monetization approach (within SUITE ecosystem)
