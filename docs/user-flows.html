🚶 User Flows

Step-by-step journeys through the SUITE ecosystem.

💡 TL;DR: SUITE has multiple entry points. Whether you want to use apps, build apps, earn tokens, or participate in governance - there's a path for you.

🆓 Free User Flow (Earn)

Watch Ads → Get SUITE → Use Apps

No payment required. Earn tokens by watching short video ads.

📺 Watch Ad → 🪙 Get 2 SUITE → 📱 Use Apps
  • Go to Earn page
  • Click "Watch Ad" to view a 30-second video
  • Receive 2 SUITE directly to your wallet
  • Repeat - no daily limit!

💰 Buyer Flow (Deposit)

Deposit ETH → Get SUITE → Use Anywhere

Convert ETH to SUITE tokens. 1:1 backing, always redeemable.

💎 Deposit ETH → 🏦 Vault Holds → 🪙 Receive SUITE
  • Connect wallet on Wallet page
  • Enter ETH amount and click Deposit
  • ETH goes to Treasury Vault, you get SUITE minted
  • Withdraw anytime - SUITE burns, ETH returns

🌉 Bridge Flow (Wallet → Discord)

Deposit SUITE → Credit Discord → Build Apps

Transfer SUITE from your wallet to Discord to use for building apps.

🦊 Connect Wallet → 💬 Connect Discord → 🌉 Deposit SUITE → 📱 Build Apps
  • Go to Wallet page
  • Connect your MetaMask wallet
  • Scroll to "Wallet ↔ Discord Bridge" section
  • Connect your Discord account
  • Enter SUITE amount and click "→ Discord"
  • Confirm transaction in MetaMask
  • SUITE appears in your Discord balance within seconds!
💡 Why Bridge?

Your wallet holds on-chain SUITE for trading/staking. Your Discord balance is used for building apps. The bridge lets you transfer between them!

🛠️ Builder Flow (Create Apps)

Describe App → AI Builds → Publish → Earn

Build apps without coding. Just describe what you want!

💬 Describe Idea → 🤖 AI Builds → 🚀 Publish → 💸 Earn SUITE
  • Go to Build page
  • Join Discord and describe your app in #app-ideas
  • AI generates your app - iterate until perfect
  • Request publish - team reviews and approves
  • Earn SUITE when users interact with your app!

🗳️ Governance Flow (Vote on Proposals)

Connect Wallet → Vote → Shape SUITE

Your SUITE tokens = voting power. Help decide SUITE's future.

🦊 Connect Wallet → ✍️ Sign Vote → 🏆 Earn Points
  • Go to Discuss page
  • Connect your wallet (voting power = your SUITE balance)
  • Vote on proposals in the Voting column
  • Earn governance points, build your rank
  • Create proposals for 10 SUITE (Council members: free)

🚀 Incubator Flow (Launch a Project)

Apply → Get Support → Launch

Build a bigger project with SUITE backing and community support.

📝 Apply → 👥 Interview → 🛠️ Build → 🚀 Launch
  • Go to Incubate page
  • Submit your project idea
  • If accepted: get mentorship, funding, and community support
  • Build and launch with the SUITE ecosystem behind you

🛒 Store Flow (Buy Boosts)

Browse → Buy → Power Up

Purchase powerups and items to supercharge your apps.

🛒 Browse Store → 💳 Pay SUITE → ⚡ Get Boosted
  • Go to Boost page
  • Connect your wallet
  • Browse available powerups
  • Purchase with SUITE tokens

🔄 The Complete Cycle

The SUITE flywheel:

Users → Apps → Builders earn → More apps → More users → Treasury grows → Charity impact 💝
