Guidelines for apps, content, and behavior in the SUITE ecosystem.

üåü Our Mission

SUITE is a platform where anyone can build apps and everyone gets paid fairly. We aim to create a safe, inclusive, and innovative ecosystem for creators and users alike.

‚úÖ Acceptable Content

We welcome apps and content that:

  • Provide value - Productivity tools, games, utilities, creative apps, educational content
  • Respect users - Clear about data usage, honest about features, no dark patterns
  • Follow platform guidelines - Compatible with app store policies (Apple/Google)
  • Are original - Your own work or properly licensed content

üö´ Prohibited Content

The following content is not allowed and will result in app removal:

Explicit/Adult Content

No pornographic material, explicit sexual content, or nudity. This includes AI-generated adult content.

Illegal Activity

No apps promoting or facilitating illegal activities including fraud, hacking, drug sales, or piracy.

Hate & Harassment

No content promoting violence, discrimination, or harassment based on race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics.

Harmful Content

No malware, spyware, phishing, scams, or apps designed to deceive or harm users.

Child Safety

Zero tolerance for any content that exploits, endangers, or sexualizes minors. Violations will be reported to authorities.

Intellectual Property

No copyright infringement, trademark violations, or impersonation of other apps/brands.

ü§ñ AI-Generated Content

Apps using AI must comply with these additional guidelines:

  • Use safe AI providers - Only use AI APIs with built-in content filters (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google)
  • No jailbreaking - Don't circumvent AI safety filters or guardrails
  • Disclosure - Be transparent when content is AI-generated
  • Moderation - Implement additional content filtering for user-generated AI prompts

‚öñÔ∏è Enforcement

SUITE enforces these policies through:

1. App Review

All apps go through manual review before being published. Apps that violate policies will be rejected with feedback.

2. Community Reporting

Users can report apps that violate our policies. Reports are reviewed within 24-48 hours.

3. Consequences

  • First violation - App removed, warning issued
  • Second violation - Account suspended for 30 days
  • Severe/repeat violations - Permanent ban from the platform
  • Illegal content - Immediate permanent ban and report to authorities

üì¢ Reporting Violations

To report an app or user violating these policies:

  • Use the "Report App" button on any app page
  • Contact us in the SUITE Discord
  • Email: reports@getsuite.app
