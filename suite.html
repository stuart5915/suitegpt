?? Fees & Examples

Transparent fee structure with simple examples

Minting (Deposit)

0.5% fee
Example:
You deposit: $1,000 USDC

You receive: 995 $SUITE

Redemption (Withdraw)

0.5% fee + 7 day wait
Example:
You redeem: 1,000 $SUITE
Current backing: $1.10 per token
Gross value: $1,100
Redeem fee (0.5%): -$5.50
You receive (after 7 days): $1,094.50 USDC
7-day cooldown prevents bank runs, protects treasury

App Usage

Tokens burned ? supply decreases ? backing increases for holders

Why These Fees?

The 0.5% mint + 0.5% redeem fees create a ~1% premium band on DEXs. Arbitrage bots keep the market price near treasury backing value, while fees accumulate in the treasury to benefit all holders.
