## Quick Setup Checklist

### Equipment (Free/Low Cost)
- [ ] **OBS Studio** - Free screen recorder (download: obsproject.com)
- [ ] **Microphone** - Even a $30 USB mic is a huge upgrade over laptop mic
- [ ] **Good lighting** - Face a window or use a desk lamp
- [ ] **Quiet room** - Turn off fans, close windows

### OBS Settings
```
Video: 1920x1080 (1080p)
FPS: 30
Encoder: x264 or NVENC (if you have Nvidia GPU)
Audio: 48kHz
```

## Video Structure Template

### 1. Hook (0-10 sec)
> "In this video, I'll show you how to [RESULT] in under [X] minutes"

- Make them excited about what they'll build

### 3. Step-by-Step Tutorial (5-15 min)
- Go slow, explain each step
- Zoom in on important areas
- Pause after each major step

## Recording Tips

### Before Recording
- [ ] Close unnecessary tabs/apps
- [ ] Turn off notifications (Focus mode on Windows)
- [ ] Have your script/outline ready
- [ ] Do a 10-second test recording to check audio

### While Recording
- Speak slowly and clearly
- It's okay to pause - you can edit later
- If you mess up, just pause and redo that section
- Keep energy up - smile while talking!

### After Recording
- Watch it back at 2x speed to find mistakes
- Trim dead air at start/end
- Add simple text overlays for key points (optional)
