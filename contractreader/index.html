Frequently Asked Questions

Is this legal advice?
No. ContractReader is an AI analysis tool that helps you understand contracts in plain English. It is not a substitute for qualified legal counsel. Always consult a lawyer for important legal decisions.
Is my contract data secure?
Your PDF text is extracted entirely in your browser. The raw file never leaves your device. Only the extracted text is sent for AI analysis over an encrypted connection. We do not store your contracts.
What types of contracts work?
Any text-based PDF: residential and commercial leases, employment contracts, NDAs, freelance agreements, vendor contracts, terms of service, partnership agreements, and more.
How accurate is the analysis?
ContractReader uses advanced AI to identify common contractual patterns and potential issues. It catches things most people miss, but should be used as a starting point — not a replacement for professional legal review on high-stakes agreements.
How much does it cost?
ContractReader uses 5 SUITE credits per analysis. New accounts start with free credits. Create a free SUITE account to get started.
Can I ask questions about specific clauses?
Yes. After the initial analysis, use the Q&A tab to ask anything: "Can I terminate early?" "What happens if I'm late on rent?" "Is the non-compete enforceable?" The AI answers based on the actual contract text.
