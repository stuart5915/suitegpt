Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our putters and ordering process.

Our Rough Mill is CNC-milled from solid 304 stainless steel, known for its durability and corrosion resistance. The Centre Blade is crafted from 1045 carbon steel, which provides a softer feel preferred by many tour players. Both materials are precision-machined at our facility in Canada.

The Rough Mill is available in Raw (showing the natural machined texture), Brushed, Black DLC (Diamond-Like Carbon coating), and Chrome. The Centre Blade comes standard with a gun blue finish and white sight line paint fill. Finish upgrades may affect pricing.

Our standard putters come at 35" length. Custom length and lie angle adjustments may be available for an additional fee. Please contact us to discuss custom options before placing your order.

All putters come with a SuperStroke Pistol 2.0 style grip. The Rough Mill offers the grip in standard colors, while the Centre Blade comes with an all-black grip to complement its aesthetic. Custom grip options may be available upon request.

Standard shipping within Canada and the US typically takes 5-7 business days. International shipping times vary by destination. All orders are carefully packaged to ensure your putter arrives safely.

Yes! Local pickup is available at our Cambridge, Ontario location. Select "Local Pickup" during checkout and we'll contact you to schedule an appointment. Pickup is free of charge.

We accept all major credit cards through Stripe, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. Apple Pay and Google Pay are also available at checkout.

We stand behind the quality of our putters. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact us within 30 days of delivery. Custom-finished putters may have different return terms. Please see our full return policy or contact us for details.

Keep your putter dry and clean after each round. For stainless steel models, wipe with a soft cloth. Carbon steel putters with gun blue finish should be occasionally treated with light oil to maintain the finish and prevent rust. Always use a headcover to protect the face from dings.

Yes, we offer refinishing services for Proto Golf putters. If your putter needs attention after heavy use, contact us to discuss refinishing options and pricing. We can restore your putter to like-new condition.
