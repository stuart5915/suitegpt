About Proto Golf

Precision engineering meets AI innovation to help every golfer find their perfect fit.

Our Story

Proto Golf was born from a simple observation: most golfers play with equipment that doesn't match their swing. We set out to change that by combining traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge AI technology.

Based in Cambridge, Massachusetts, we're part of Telos Manufacturing's commitment to American-made precision equipment. Every putter we produce is CNC-milled in our facility, ensuring consistent quality and performance.

P

Precision Milling

Our putters are precision-milled from premium materials using state-of-the-art CNC machines. This process ensures every club meets our exacting standards for face flatness, weight distribution, and overall quality.

Unlike cast putters, our milled designs offer consistent grain structure throughout the head, resulting in a softer feel and more predictable ball roll.

AI-Powered Fitting

Our proprietary AI swing analyzer uses computer vision to evaluate your stroke in real-time. By analyzing key positions and movements, we can recommend equipment that complements your natural swing.

No more guessing or generic recommendations. Our technology provides personalized insights based on your unique biomechanics.
