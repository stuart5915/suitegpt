Our Story

Precision Craftsmanship

Built on a foundation of design, machining, and a serious love for the links, Proto Golf delivers premium Canadian-made gear to the market. We apply the same tight tolerances to our manufacturing that we aim for in our short game, creating quality products that truly hit the sweet spot.

What started as a passion project became something more. We wanted to create putters that combined traditional craftsmanship with modern manufacturing precision. Every Proto Golf putter is machined in-house, from solid metal stock to finished product.

Process

How We Build

1

Material Selection

We source premium 304 stainless steel mined in Canada in solid stock form. No castings, no compromises.

2

CNC Machining

Each putter head is precision CNC-milled from a single piece of metal. This ensures consistency and eliminates the weaknesses found in multi-piece construction.

3

Finishing

Whether raw, brushed, DLC coated, or chromed, each finish is applied with care. Gun blue finishes are hand-applied for that classic patina.

Manufacturing

Manufacturing

Proto Golf is proudly made at Telos Manufacturing in Cambridge, Ontario. Our facility houses multiple CNC machines capable of tight-tolerance work across various materials.

CNC

Precision Equipment

Multi-axis CNC mills and lathes allow us to produce complex geometries with exceptional accuracy.

QC

Quality Control

Every putter is inspected for weight, balance, and dimensional accuracy before leaving our facility.

CAN

Made in Canada

From design to delivery, everything happens locally. We're proud to manufacture in Canada.
