Bonus Credits & Redemption

When you buy credits, you receive bonus credits on top of your purchase. These bonus credits can be withdrawn for USDC based on the current redemption rate.

Understanding the Redemption Rate

The redemption rate shows what percentage of face value your bonus credits are worth. For example, if the rate is 85%, you can withdraw $0.85 for every $1 of bonus credits.

This rate changes based on how the SUITE reward pool performs. It can go up or down.

How Redemption Works

1
Request Withdrawal Enter how much USDC you want to withdraw (up to your max redeemable)
2
Request Queued Your request enters a queue while funds are prepared
3
Funds Available Once the pool is funded, you can claim your USDC
4
Claim USDC Click "Claim" to receive USDC directly to your wallet

Why the Rate Changes

The redemption rate reflects the real value backing your bonus credits:

Rate Goes Up
When the reward pool grows (from yield, revenue, etc.), the rate increases and your bonus credits are worth more.
Rate Goes Down
If the pool value decreases or more bonus credits are issued, the rate may decrease temporarily.
Key Point: Only bonus credits can be withdrawn. Credits you purchase directly are for using apps - they're not withdrawable because you're buying services, not making an investment.
