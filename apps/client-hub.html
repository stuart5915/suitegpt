Where to Find Clients
Walk-In (Local Businesses)
Best conversion rate. Walk into shops, restaurants, barbershops, gyms, trades businesses. Show them Proto Golf on your phone as a demo. They can see it's real. Target businesses that: - Have a bad website or no website - Do things manually (paper, spreadsheets) - Have inventory to manage - Would benefit from online presence
Fiverr / Upwork
Good for getting first clients. Create a gig: "I'll build you a custom AI-powered website + admin dashboard" Price the setup at $0 (free) and offer a monthly maintenance package. This gets you in the door. Once they see the value, they stay. Search for people requesting: website redesign, admin dashboard, inventory management, small business website.
Social Media
Post on X/Twitter and LinkedIn: - Show what you built for Proto Golf (with permission) - Before/after comparisons - Screen recordings of the chat interface - "Built a custom AI dashboard for a golf company. They manage their entire business through a chat. Looking to do this for 5 more businesses. Free setup, free first month." Post in r/smallbusiness, r/entrepreneur, Facebook groups for local business owners.
Referrals
Ask Proto Golf to refer you. Offer them a discount or a free month for every referral that converts. Every happy client should be asked: "Know anyone else who could use something like this?" This is the highest quality lead source once you have 2-3 clients.
