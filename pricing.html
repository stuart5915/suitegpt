Frequently Asked Questions

What is 2x earn rate?
Pro subscribers earn double $SUITE tokens on all earning activities - watching ads, completing surveys, trying apps, and more. Same effort, double rewards!
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! Cancel anytime from your account. You'll keep Pro benefits until the end of your billing period.
What happens to my apps if I downgrade?
Your published apps stay live. You just can't add more than 1 active development app on the free plan.

No! Free builders can publish apps too. Pro just gives you faster reviews and more development slots.
Is this a subscription for $SUITE tokens?
No. You're paying for platform features (faster reviews, analytics, more apps). $SUITE is earned through activity - Pro just gives you a 2x multiplier on earnings.
