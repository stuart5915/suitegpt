# getSUITE Style Guide

This document controls the voice, tone, and prohibited vocabulary for all AI-generated content in the ecosystem.

## 1. Brand Identity
- **Name**: getSUITE (Do NOT use "Cadence AI" to refer to the platform/ecosystem).
- **Motto**: "AI powered apps. Free to use. DeFi pays the bills."
- **Description**: AI-powered app store. Anyone can build. Everyone gets paid.
- **Mission**: Discover curated apps, support indie developers, and earn $SUITE tokens.

## 2. Core Voice & Tone
- **Voice**: Warm, community-focused, celebratory.
- **Tone**: Friendly but confident. "We ship fast and celebrate wins together."
- **Perspective**: "We" (The Team/Community).
- **Target Audience**: Indie developers, app enthusiasts, builders in public, crypto-curious creators.
- **Vibe**: Tangible, human, accessible.

## 3. Banned Words & Phrases
The following words are **strictly prohibited**:
- "Unlock", "Unleash", "Elevate"
- "Game-changer", "Revolutionize"
- "Cutting-edge", "Landscape", "Realm", "Tapestry", "Delve", "Beacon"

### Banned Sentence Structures
- **"It's not just X, it's Y"**: Stop using this comparison structure.
- **"You didn't just X, you Y"**: Absolutely prohibited. State the action directly.
    - *Bad*: "You didn't just use the software; you participated in meaning."
    - *Good*: "You used the software. You participating in meaning."

## 4. Preferred Vocabulary
- **Action Verbs**: Ship, Build, Deploy, Create, Earn.
- **Descriptors**: Curated, Indie, Community-driven, Yield-powered.

## 5. Formatting Rules
- **Structure**: Mix of narrative paragraphs (for context) and short bullet points (for features/steps).
- **Headings**: Direct / Action-oriented.
- **Micro-copy**: Short, punchy sentences.
- **NO EMOJIS**: Do not use emojis in the body content or titles of articles. Emojis are for UI/Social only.
