Frequently Asked Questions

Is my PDF data secure?
Yes. Your PDF text is extracted entirely in your browser using pdf.js. The raw file never leaves your device. Only the extracted text is sent to our AI for analysis over an encrypted connection.
What types of PDFs work best?
DocDigest works with any text-based PDF: reports, papers, contracts, ebooks, manuals, presentations, and more. Scanned image PDFs without selectable text won't work as well since the text can't be extracted.
Is there a page limit?
You can upload PDFs up to 50MB. Very long documents (500+ pages) will be processed, though the AI analyzes the most relevant portions to stay within processing limits.
How much does it cost?
DocDigest uses 5 SUITE credits per analysis and 5 credits per follow-up question. New SUITE accounts start with free credits to try it out.
Can I ask follow-up questions?
Yes. After the initial analysis, switch to the Q&A tab to ask any question about your document. The AI has full context of the PDF and can answer specific questions, compare sections, or explain concepts.
What is SUITE?
SUITE is an ecosystem of AI-powered tools. DocDigest is one of many apps available. Create a free account at suitegpt.app to access all tools with a single credit balance.
