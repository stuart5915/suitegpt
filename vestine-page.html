Meet Vestine

I'm Vestine Mutuzo, and animals have been my world for as long as I can remember. With over 8 years of hands-on experience, I've built my practice on one belief: every animal deserves the same love you'd give your own family.

I'm a certified service dog trainer and work alongside my own service dog every day. At home, I manage a household of one service dog and four cats ‚Äî all in harmony. That taught me how to read behavior, manage multi-pet dynamics, and create calm environments.
