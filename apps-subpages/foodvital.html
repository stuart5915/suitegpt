About FoodVital

Transform your nutrition tracking with AI. Simply snap a photo of any meal and FoodVital instantly analyzes the ingredients, calculates macros (protein, carbs, fats), and logs it to your daily journal. No more guessing portion sizes or manually searching food databases - our AI does all the heavy lifting.

Get personalized meal suggestions based on your remaining daily goals, track your micronutrients, and receive AI-powered insights on nutritional gaps. Whether you're bulking, cutting, or maintaining, FoodVital keeps you on track with minimal effort.
